About Me

I’m a dedicated software developer with a solid foundation in Chemistry, bringing precision and attention to detail from the lab to the world of web development. Raised in a Nigerian family, I carry with me the vibrant energy, creativity, and resourcefulness that define the "Naija" spirit. My journey into tech started with a deep curiosity for problem-solving, and that drive has led me to contribute to impactful projects, including HBB.chat, a real-time communication platform, that enable influencers make interact with their fans and also generate income..

I believe in creating solutions that don’t just work, but that make an impact — blending technical expertise with a touch of Nigerian flair. Whether it's in front-end design or back-end architecture, I approach every challenge with determination, a bit of that Naija hustle, and a commitment to building innovative, user-friendly solutions.
